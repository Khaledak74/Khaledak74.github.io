[CyberDefenders] Phishy Lab

Endpoint forensics investigation on a disk image, reconstructing a full phishing attack chain: a malicious Word document delivered via WhatsApp, a macro that executed PowerShell, a downloaded Meterpreter payload, and the attacker's C2 connecting back — plus credential theft via a fake Apple login page.

FTK Imager Registry Explorer DB Browser for SQLite OleDump olevba CyberChef VirusTotal Any.run BrowserHistoryView PasswordFox
The full phishing chain lives in artifacts you wouldn't think to look at — the download URL was in a WhatsApp SQLite database, the C2 IP was only visible through Any.run's sandbox behavioral analysis (not VirusTotal static detection), and the victim's browser stored a plaintext password in an encrypted logins.json that PasswordFox decrypted in seconds.
